Ideology

Dream Big I believe in keeping my inner child alive by dreaming big. I try my hardest to not to put any limits or constraints on my dreams -- regardless of how "unrealistic" they might seem.

Be Optimistic Being positive is essential to success and growth but has been the hardest for me to maintain. I intend to keep adjusting my perspectives and approaches to align with this.

Progress > Perfection For a long time I struggled with getting things right on the first try. Failure wasn't an option -- an unhealthy belief. Focusing on progress gives me the space to grow and thrive at my own pace.

Be Patient In the era where everything seems to happen instantaneously, it can sometimes be hard to practice patience. Practising patience allows me to slow down and appreciate the journey.
